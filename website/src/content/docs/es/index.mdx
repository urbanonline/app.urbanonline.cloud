## App Features

<CardGrid stagger>
	<Card title="Hierarchical Barn Structure" icon="farcaster" >
		Create your `Barns` and assign `Feeders` to them.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Filters: Barns, Feeders" icon="question-circle">
		View the animals from all barns, from a specific barn, or from a specific feeder.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Animal search" icon="magnifier">
		`Search` for a specific animal in your herd list using its `animal number`.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Individual animal information" icon="information">
		Scroll through the individual animals and view the animal info or the feed overview.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Operating status of feeders" icon="analytics">
		View the `operating status` of all feeders, including any `error messages`.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Push notification in case of feeder errors" icon="comment-alt">
		If an error occurs at a feeder, you will be notified via a `push notification`.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Flexible user management" icon="setting">
		Create as many `co-users` as you like and invite them to use the app together with you.
	</Card>
</CardGrid>
