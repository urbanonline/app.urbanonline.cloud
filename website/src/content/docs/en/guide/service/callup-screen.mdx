To begin the installation, you must first open the installation screen for the Urban Cloud service:

### Opening the installation screen

<Tabs>
<TabItem label="Quick version">
Click, as shown in [Screenshot 1](#screenshot1) below, on buttons <span class="text-2xl">➀</span>&nbsp;through&nbsp;<span class="text-2xl">⑤</span> in sequence.
</TabItem>
<TabItem label="Detailed description">
1. In the left-hand sidebar of the touch terminal, click the bottom button <Gear width={20} class="inline ml-2 mb-0" /> `Settings` <span class="text-2xl">➀</span>. If you are in the main menu, where no sidebar is visible, press the button <Feeder width={20} class="inline ml-2 mb-0" /> `Feeder` to display the sidebar.
1. In the bottom toolbar of the touch terminal, click the second button from the left <TouchGear width={30} class="inline ml-2 mb-0" /> `Touch-Terminal Settings` <span class="text-2xl">➁</span>.
1. A series of menu items now appears in the left half of the central screen area. Click the bottom button `Accessories` <span class="text-2xl">➂</span>.
1. One (or possibly several) menu item(s) now appears in the right half of the central screen area. Click the bottom button `Urban Cloud Service` <span style="font-size: 140%" id="Navigation_Digit_4">➃</span>. The button now moves to the left half of the screen.
1. At the bottom of the right half of the screen, the button <Edit width={20} class="inline ml-2 mb-0" /> `Open settings` <span style="font-size: 140%" id="Navigation_Digit_5">➄</span> is now displayed. Click this button; a popup window opens listing the [steps](../setup-cloud) for setting up the Urban Cloud service.
</TabItem>
</Tabs>
