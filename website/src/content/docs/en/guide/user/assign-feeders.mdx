## Requirements

- iPhone with iOS (currently available for iOS only)
- Access to the App Store
- Your Urban account credentials (for new registrations: email address and company details)

---

## 1. Registration

Launch the Urban App. Complete the short introduction or skip this step.

<Image src={Einfuehrung} alt="Urban App introduction screen" width={300} />

Tap **Register** to create a new account and set up your farm.

<Image src={Registrierung} alt="Login page with Register link" width={300} />

Enter your account details and accept the terms of use.

<Image src={Accountdaten} alt="Create account form" width={300} />

Enter your company details. Tap **Continue** to complete the registration. You will receive a confirmation email at the address you provided.

<Image src={Unternehmensdaten} alt="Company details form" width={300} />

---

## 2. Log in

Log in with your credentials.

<Image src={Login} alt="Login screen" width={300} />

---

## 3. Set up a barn

Tap **Add a new barn**.

<Image src={StallAnlegen} alt="Add barn" width={300} />

Enter a name for your barn and confirm with **Save**.

<Image src={StallBenennen} alt="Name barn" width={300} />

---

## 4. Add a terminal

Go to the TouchTerminal you want to add and open the following menu.

{/* Image: Screenshot TouchTerminal menu */}

Tap **Scan QR code** and scan the QR code on the TouchTerminal you want to add.

<Image src={QRCodeScannen} alt="Scan QR code" width={300} />

The app automatically adds the feeders of the TouchTerminal. Synchronisation may take a few minutes. If necessary, log out and back in after 5 minutes.

The app will now display the added feeders, which must be licensed before use.

{/* Image: Unlicensed feeders – graphic pending */}

---

## 5. License feeders

Navigate to **Settings → Subscription**.

Here you can see an overview of your active licenses and monthly costs. Tap **Upgrade my plan**.

<Image src={Abonnement} alt="Subscription overview" width={300} />

In the license management, select the feeders you want to license and confirm with **Save**.

<Image src={Lizenzverwaltung} alt="License management" width={300} />

Confirm your selection and complete the payment process. You will then be redirected back to the app.

<Image src={Zahlvorgang} alt="Payment process" width={300} />

---

## 6. Assign feeders to a barn

Navigate to **Settings → Feeders → Edit barn**.

Select the licensed feeders and confirm with **Save**.

<Image src={LizensierteAutomaten} alt="Licensed feeders" width={300} />

---

## Setup complete

You can now view your animal data.

<Image src={Abschluss} alt="Dashboard with animal data" width={300} />
